Abstract

Read Full Paper

AdditiveNet is a benchmark for metal additive manufacturing machine learning, utilizing machine learning methods on additive manufacturing in designing, building, process and post-process monitoring as well as testing and validation. AdditiveNet curates a number of dataset collections, building a suite of software implementing many known featurizations and algorithms. All methods and datasets are integrated into the open source AdditiveNet package. AdditiveNet provides a series of benchmark results of implemented various machine learning models with different featurizations on datasets. The results reported in AUC-ROC, AUC-PRC, RMSE and MAE scores will be maintained online in an ongoing manner.
